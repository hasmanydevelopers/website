About us  It's Nice to Meet You!

Welcome to has_many :developers

After near 10 years of corporate Java development we jumped into Ruby On Rails in 2006, http://rubycorner.com/ was our first Ruby On Rails application, and it is still listed on ruby-lang in the Community section. And after 7 years of experience with RoR we still love it.

Rails is the foundation on which we have been building SPAs (Single Page Applications) in the last couple years, on top of it we have tried: Spine.js, Backbone.js and Angular.js whcis has been a really exciting experience.

Our team has changed over the years, good friends have left us and now are working at companies like Groupon and Zillow, but something has remained: our commitment grow each of our teammates as full stack web developers fond on the latest trends on web development together with the best Agile practices.
