Project Description

Pickista is a fun and simple way to ask a question. A visual poll that lets you get the answers you need, when you need them, from the people you trust. Using images to explain your dilemma in a nutshell, you can send your question to your most trusted advisors from anywhere on the web. With lightning-quick speed, we’ll tally their votes (or “picks”) for your perusal. The result? Goodbye, pickle. Hello, next steps...
